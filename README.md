## Features | ‚ûï
**Every feature mentioned in this mod is configurable.**

- Kill players (or entities if configured) to gain a configurable amount of permanent health!
- Configure the amount of max and min health for players!
- When players lose all their max health, they are banned from the server
- A new special weapon that siphons health from your enemies to heal you when you attack them! (WORK IN PROGRESS)
- Two ways to revive players!
  - Through a one use item that allows you to revive anyone you want (WORK IN PROGRESS)
  - Automatic revive timer (configurable!)
- Special ores underground that allow you to craft heart crystals to gain even more permanent health! Mauhahaha! (WORK IN PROGRESS)

## Config | ‚öôÔ∏è

- StartAmount : The amount of extra stolen health people start with
- StealAmount : The amount of health people steal when they kill an entity
- CapAmount : Limits the amount of stolen health a player can have, set to -1 to disable
- MinAmount : Limits the amount of debt a player can have in stolen health
- BanAtMin : Whether to ban players when they reach the debt limit or not
- AutoReviveAfterTime : Automatically revives players after a certain amount of seconds
- LoseHealthOnEnvDeath : Whether to lose health on environment death
- LoseHealthOnPlayerDeath : Whether to lose health on player death
- LoseHealthOnEntityDeath : Whether to lose health on entity death
- EarnLifeFromNonPlayers : Whether to earn stolen health from non-players
- CanEarnLifeFromEntitiesEvenIfTheyDontHaveEnough : Whether to earn stolen health from entities even if they reached the debt limit
